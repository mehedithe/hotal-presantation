Sayeman Beach Restaurant & Bar

(In Room Dinning 24 Hours)

Restaurants & Bar at Sayeman turns up the heat on culinary glamour. Dine, drink and chill at our fabulous dining destinations, all overseen by our team of culinary expert lead by internationally experienced Chef & his team. Savor creative cocktails and delectable dishes at our four can’t-miss Sayeman restaurants – Casablanca Restaurant, Surfside Café, YuMe Infinity Pool Café and Sunset Bar & Grill.
Along with the sublime food, you can sip cocktails by the sea in one of the best seaside bar Cox’s Bazar at Sunset Bar & Grill. You have a choice of sitting by the extended deck overlooking the ocean and sea beach or sit inside a cozy atmosphere listening to live music. If you are in the mood of letting yourself lose on the dance floor then you must visit and dance to the DJ’s beat at Carnival Lounge.
